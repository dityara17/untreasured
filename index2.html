What is Karaoke Heaven ?

Karaoke heaven is a community ran discord server, with the intention of allowing community member to run their own karaoke event and have a load of fun with the community and their friends/ We have created a wide range of tools to allow you to manage your own event. We work tirelessly around the clock to ensure that our discord and website are amazing as possible. If you have any suggestion: feel free to put the in the #suggestion channel on our discord
